All sessions are conducted via **Microsoft Teams**. To ensure the highest standard of care and professional development, sessions are recorded.

* **Purpose of Recording:** Recordings are used exclusively for **internal clinical review** by senior consultants to ensure quality of care and professional supervision.

* **Storage:** Recordings are stored in an encrypted, password-protected environment within our professional Microsoft 365 Business ecosystem.

### 4. Data Storage and Location (India & International)

As we are located in **Bangalore, India**, your data is processed in accordance with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023**.

* **International Clients:** For clients located in the EU/UK or other regions, we implement "Standard Contractual Clauses" to ensure your data receives the same level of protection as it would under **GDPR**.
* **Data Residency:** While we are based in India, Microsoft Teams data may be processed on servers globally as per Microsoft’s enterprise privacy standards.

### 5. Access and Confidentiality

Confidentiality is the cornerstone of our practice. Access to your recordings and notes is strictly limited to:

1. Your primary consultant.
2. Designated senior clinical supervisors (for internal review purposes only).
3. Legal authorities only when mandated by a court order or to prevent immediate physical harm to yourself or others.

**Your recordings are never sold, shared with third-party advertisers, or used for public marketing.**
